When you get stuck

Two moves that replace hacking.

The second you hit a wall, you stop and pick one. No more burning an hour grinding bad reps into your hands.

Method 01

The Atomic Breakdown

Isolate the smallest piece that's actually bottlenecking you: measure, to beat, to rudiment, to note, to stroke type. Fix that one piece on its own, then put it back in context. Attack the root, not the symptom.

Method 02

The Atomic Slowdown

There's always a speed you can play it clean. Slow down until you literally can't make a mistake, seal the cracks in your foundation, then build the speed back gradually. Playing slow is the fastest way to get fast.
