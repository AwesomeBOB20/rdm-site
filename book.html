Stop repeating the same three warm-ups

You know the feeling. You sit down at the pad, run the exercises you always run, and nothing gets harder. Gridding fixes that. It takes a rhythm you already know and varies it in ways that force your hands, your reading, and your brain to keep up.

One basic exercise becomes hundreds of new ones. That is the whole trick, and this book teaches all of it.

  • The 6 fundamental grid types: Exercise, AB, 4-2-1, Pyramid, 7/8, and Shopping Sprees
  • The 12 gridding variations, from Off The Left through Backwards and Reverse
  • Variation tables in every chapter, so you can build your own grids for years
  • Combination grids at the end of each chapter that stack variations into real challenges
